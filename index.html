Lahore Grammar School (LGS)

LGS is a well-known private school in Pakistan, founded in 1979 in Lahore. It offers local and international curricula (Matric, O-Levels, A-Levels) and focuses on academic excellence, English proficiency, and co-curricular activities like sports and debates. LGS aims to develop confident, disciplined, and responsible students.

Address: Lahore Grammar School, 18 Awaisia, WAPDA Town, Lahore, Punjab, Pakistan

Enrollment: LGS has more than 87 campuses in Pakistan and serves over 50,000 students total.

Streams Offered: Science, Commerce, and Humanities (Arts) streams with subjects like Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, Accounting, Business Studies, Economics, English, Urdu, and Information Technology.

Educational Board: Registered with the Cambridge International Examinations (CIE) for O Levels and A Levels, and many branches also follow the Punjab Education Board/Matric system.
